About India Hackathon Hub

Our Mission

We believe that talent is distributed, but opportunities are concentrated. India Hackathon Hub is built to connect passionate developers, designers, and innovators with the best hackathon opportunities across the country. We're on a mission to democratize access to innovation competitions and help the next generation of tech leaders build something amazing.

✨ Key Features

Smart Search

Find hackathons by name, organizer, location, and more with our advanced search and filtering system.

Save & Organize

Bookmark your favorite hackathons and keep them organized in one place for easy access.

Community Ratings

Rate hackathons and read authentic reviews from community members to make informed decisions.

Share & Spread

Real-time Updates

Get notified about new hackathons, deadline reminders, and important announcements instantly.

PWA Ready
